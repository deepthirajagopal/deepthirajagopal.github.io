About Me

I grew up in Southern part of India, learning art, dance and science. I have always dreamt of being a designer where art meets technology and science. I pursued my dream to learn design from NIFT, India. and then working in the industry got me really intrigued about the potential of technology and design. So, currently I am pursuing MS in HCI from Depaul University.

As a Designer, I enjoy problem solving and reaching a delightful solution by deliberate understanding of the problem through research and analysis. I have first hand experience in both UI/UX research and design. I embody strong empathy with design thinking in my work and advocate delivering value to users. My background has its core in design research to identify problems. Currently, I am pursuing my MS in Human-Computer Interaction and passionate towards continuing in the field of User research and Design.

Courses taken in MS-HCI program:

  • IT-403 Statistics and Data Analysis
  • HCI-440 Intro to User Centered Design
  • HCI-406 Website design for Human Computer Interaction
  • HCI-450 Foundations of Hman Computer Interaction
