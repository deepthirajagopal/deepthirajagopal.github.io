Android themes

UI Design

Product & Market

The Xolo range of devices at Lava International Ltd. are targeted at users from 18 - 25 years of age primarily. I was a part of the team which created the custom ROM-HIVE for all Xolo Android devices. My role was to create custom UI themes and share it through an digital content sharing application -"Edge".

User study

A study of Xolo Black device target users and their behaviors were mapped using the XOLO- HIVE user forum and demographic studies. A color theme desplaying the soothing evenings was chosen based on the analytics of previously performing themes.

Form study and Inspiration

The form study for the icons were taken from old school space ornamented with telephones, typewriters and coffee tables. These forms were used to create icons and assets.

Theme and UI Kit

The theme here portrays the lazy mornings with a cup of coffee. The mood of the theme is to create pleasant and relaxing vibes to the interface. The kit includes lockscreen, assets, wallpaper, widgets and icon sets.
