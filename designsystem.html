MICE Design system

Building a design system

Introduction

Starting work as a one of the first UX designer for MICE Portal GmbH, it was extremely challenging to work on new concepts and design without any branding or design guideline in place. All the previous agency and design work was lost or misplaced without any trace of history making it difficult with ongoing fast paced design work.

Problem definition

  • New visual design concepts were to be built from scratch leading to inconsistency in design
  • Marketing design and UX design deliverables varied to large extents
  • No visual language and consistency in place across other B2C applications

Users

Primary: UX Designer, Graphic designer
Secondary: Front end developer
Tertiary: Marketing, sales and Customer care

Team & role

Role: UX Designer
Team: Front end Developer

Process

  • Getting approval for allocation of time
  • Definition of metrics to show progress Eg: Improvement in time taken for new idea prototype implementation
  • Research on how other organizations are working on design system
  • Gathering existing print, digital marketing and front end design elements
  • Categorization of existing patterns into Atomic Design format
  • Building a single source - sketch file based on Atomic Design
  • Collaborating with front end developer to bring the implementation
  • Measurement of valueAdaption rate of front-end dev to follow the guidelines (Qualitative), Faster on-boarding of new designers (Qualitative), Time taken for prototyping new ideas (Quantitative)
